About Us

TukarBaju is a campaign to overcome textile waste which focuses on promoting recycling and educating the public regarding the importance of responsible consumption and sustainable textile waste management.

TukarBaju offers a platform that facilitates the public to get involved in reducing textile waste through recycling activities, educational initiatives, and environmental events
